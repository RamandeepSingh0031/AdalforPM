# Pawprint — Updated OKRs & Quarterly Roadmap (March 2026)

**Document Purpose:** Define strategic goals and quarterly milestones grounded in current March 2026 performance data.  
**Current State:** March 9, 2026 (Series A, 18-month runway)  
**Objective:** Reach profitability + Series B readiness by Q4 2026

---

## Executive Summary

As of March 2026, Pawprint is scaling but facing "softer than expected" vet utilization. Our primary focus is now the AI Symptom Checker (Q3 Roadmap) to drive conversion and the Vet Conference Initiative to bolster our partner pipeline.

1. **Optimize Unit Economics:** Maintain $15-25 CAC while driving LTV toward $240 via repeat usage.
2. **Drive Revenue Growth:** Scale from $180K MRR to $660K+ MRR by Feb 2027.
3. **Fix Retention:** Increase 6-month user retention from 35% to 45%+.
4. **Scale & Utilize Vet Network:** Grow from 120 to 320+ partners while increasing utilization.
5. **Path to Series B:** Demonstrate 3.7x revenue growth and a defensible "AI-first" triage moat.
